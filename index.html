Projects

KUKA Robot arm automation

A robotic chemistry lab assistant is currently being developed at RBCCPS, IISC, funded by DST-INSPIRE under a specific program. The goal of this project is to create a robot that can conduct experiments independently in the field of chemistry. Equipped with advanced features, this robotic system is designed to perform a wide range of experiments, including those that involve hazardous materials. The project is being carried out with great attention to detail and safety standards, and the team of experts is committed to ensuring its success. The outcome of this project is expected to revolutionize the way experiments are conducted in chemistry labs and contribute to the advancement of research in this field.
